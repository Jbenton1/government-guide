Background

From Sergeant Major to Federal Industry

Twenty-two years in the Army taught me how the government thinks and operates. As a Sergeant Major, I led across diverse operational environments, from Theater Sustainment Commands to joint and interagency missions at Naval Surface Warfare Centers. That exposure to the full lifecycle of requirements, procurement, and execution shapes how I approach federal industry today.

Now at Siemens Digital Industries, I help industry win federal automation work. My approach is different: I work upstream with A&E firms and program offices to influence requirements before they hit the street, not downstream competing on published RFPs. That means building relationships that pay off across multiple projects, not chasing one solicitation at a time.

The other half of what I do is paying it forward for the many who invested in my own transition. The transition from a military to civilian career can be challenging. If I can help you with yours, please reach out and let me know.
