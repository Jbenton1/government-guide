USACE Veterans Curation Program

U.S. Army Corps of Engineers

One of the best-kept secrets in federal hiring. This 5-month paid training program teaches veterans to rehabilitate archaeological collections while building direct pathways to permanent federal employment. Labs in Virginia, Georgia, Missouri, and California.

You'll work with real archaeological artifacts, learn museum curation, digital imaging, and records management. Many graduates transition directly into permanent GS positions at USACE or other federal agencies. No prior experience required.

Special Hiring Authorities

Veterans Recruitment Appointment (VRA)

Non-competitive hiring for positions up to GS-11 without a vacancy announcement. Converts to career-conditional after 2 years.

Eligibility: Campaign badge, disabled, GWOT Medal, recently separated (3 yrs)
Learn more ‚Üí

30% or More Disabled Veteran Authority

Non-competitive hiring to ANY federal position at ANY grade level. One of the most powerful hiring authorities available.

Eligibility: 30%+ VA disability rating
Learn more ‚Üí

Schedule A (Disabled Veterans)

Excepted service authority for individuals with severe physical, psychiatric, or intellectual disabilities.

Eligibility: Disabled veterans with qualifying conditions
Learn more ‚Üí

VEOA (Veterans Employment Opportunities Act)

Compete for merit promotion positions normally open only to current federal employees.

Eligibility: Preference eligible OR 3+ years active service

Federal Employment Programs

Hiring Our Heroes Corporate Fellowship

12-week paid SkillBridge fellowship at Amazon, Deloitte, and Fortune 500 companies. 4 days/week at host company while maintaining military pay.

Eligibility: Active Duty (SkillBridge)
